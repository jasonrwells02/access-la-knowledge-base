# Operations Monitoring Center (OMC)

**Phone:** 800-827-0829 (Option 2)

**Hours:** 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays




## Purpose
Real-time trip issues: late vehicles, missed connections, ETAs, same-day emergencies, serious service disruptions





## Capabilities
- Request Estimated Time of Arrival (available 15 minutes before pickup)
- Missed connection/transfer issues
- No-show trip reporting
- Serious service disruptions requiring immediate response
- Secure alternate Access vehicle or backup provider

## Cannot Help With
- Eligibility questions
- Card replacement
- Coupon orders
- Service tickets
- Procedure concerns
- Travel time issues

## Caller Intents Routed Here
- **trip-late**: where's my ride, when is my ride coming, my ride is late...

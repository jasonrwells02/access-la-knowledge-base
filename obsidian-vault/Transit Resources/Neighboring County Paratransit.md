# Neighboring County Paratransit

ADA paratransit services in counties bordering Los Angeles County. Access riders can use these services as visitors for up to 21 days per calendar year. Call Access at 800-827-0829 at least two days in advance to transfer eligibility.

## Orange County
- **OCTA Access Paratransit:** 714-560-5956 (octa.net)
- **Anaheim Senior on Wheels Program:** 714-765-4510

## San Bernardino County
- **Omnitrans Access Paratransit:** 800-966-6428 (omnitrans.org)

## Ventura County
- **Gold Coast Transit Access Paratransit:** 888-667-7001 (goldcoasttransit.org)
- **VISTA (Ventura Intercity Service Transit Authority):** 805-933-2267
- **Thousand Oaks ADA Paratransit:** 805-375-5467

## Riverside County
- **RTA (Riverside Transit Agency) Dial-A-Ride:** 800-795-7887 (riversidetransit.com)

## San Diego County (Northern)
- **North County Transit District (NCTD LIFT):** 760-966-6500 (gonctd.com)
- **San Diego MTS Access:** See website (sdmts.com)

# Fares

## Standard One-Way Fares
| Trip Distance | Fare |
|---|---|
| Under 20 miles | $2.75 |
| 20+ miles | $3.5 |
| Santa Clarita / Antelope Valley | $2 |

## Special Fares
| Rider Type | Fare |
|---|---|
| Personal Care Attendant (PCA) | Free |
| Guest | Same as rider |
| Children under 6 | Free (on parent's lap) |
| Children 6+ | Pay regular fare |
| Service Animals | Free |

## Payment Methods
- **Cash**: Exact change only. Drivers cannot make change.
- **Credit/Debit Card**: Not accepted in Santa Clarita region
- **Coupon Books**: Non-refundable. Access not responsible for lost or stolen coupons.

## Coupon Books
| Type | Price | Contains | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Base Fare | $27.5 | 10 coupons at $2.75 each | Standard trips |
| Plus Zone | $7.5 | 10 coupons at $0.75 each | For trips 20+ miles (use with base fare coupon) |
| Flex | $20 | 10 coupons at $2.00 each | For Santa Clarita/Antelope Valley trips, or $2.00 toward any trip |

### Purchase Options
- **Mail:** Write Rider ID on check/money order. Send to PO Box 5728, El Monte, CA 91734. Allow 10 days for processing.
- **Online:** Order through accessla.org coupon ordering page
- **In-person:** undefined

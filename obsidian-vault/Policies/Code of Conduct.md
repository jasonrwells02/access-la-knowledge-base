# Code of Conduct

## Required Behaviors
- Seatbelt use (mandatory)
- Acceptable cleanliness standards
- Headphones for audio equipment (exception: communication devices for hearing/speech impaired)

## Prohibited
- Eating or drinking (unless medically necessary)
- Smoking or vaping
- Abusive language or behavior
- Interference with safe vehicle operation
- Harassment of riders, drivers, or employees
- Riding under alcohol or drug influence
- Hazardous materials, weapons, explosives
- Tampering with Access equipment
- Threatening communications
- Violence toward others or self

## Consequences
Violations may result in suspension. Physical assault or dangerous behavior may trigger immediate suspension and criminal prosecution.

## California Penal Codes
| Code | Offense | Penalty |
|---|---|---|
| 241.3 | Assault on transportation worker | Up to $10,000 + 5 years |
| 243.3 | Battery on transportation worker | Up to $10,000 + 5 years |
| 245.2 | Assault with deadly weapon on transportation worker | Up to $10,000 + 5 years |

> Non-violent involuntary behavior without direct threat does not result in suspension

**Video/Audio:** All vehicles equipped with cameras. Occupants are subject to recording for safety purposes.

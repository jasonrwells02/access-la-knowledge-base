# Pickup and Arrival

## On-Time Window
- **Window:** 20 minutes
- Vehicle is considered on-time if it arrives within 20 minutes of your scheduled pickup time. Example: if scheduled for 10:00 AM, vehicle may arrive between 9:40 AM and 10:20 AM.

## Driver Wait Time
- **Wait:** 5 minutes
- Driver waits 5 minutes after arrival at the curb. If driver arrives early, the 5-minute wait begins at your scheduled pickup time.

## Call-Outs
Automated phone/text notification when your vehicle is approaching
> Cannot be guaranteed. May fail with incorrect phone numbers, voicemail systems, or devices requiring extra key presses. Cell phone numbers recommended.

## Vehicle Recognition
White minivans/sedans with Access logo on doors, or certified Access taxis with logos on sides/rear. Ask driver if uncertain.

## ETA Requests
Call 800-883-1295 (press Option 4) within 15 minutes of your scheduled pickup time. You'll receive the vehicle number, type, location, and arrival estimate.

## Where's My Ride
Track vehicle location 15 minutes before scheduled pickup
- Platforms: iPhone (App Store), Android (Play Store), Computer/tablet (web), SMS text messaging
- Hotline: 213-270-6099

## Missed Trip
Call Operations Monitoring Center (OMC) at 800-827-0829 (press 2). Available 24/7. OMC secures alternate Access vehicle or backup provider.

## Curb-to-Curb Service
Access is a curb-to-curb service. Driver cannot enter buildings or private driveways.
- Rider is responsible for reaching the curb. Driver offers boarding assistance but does not lift or carry riders.

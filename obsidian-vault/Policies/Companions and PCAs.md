# Companions and PCAs

## Personal Care Attendant (PCA)
- **Fare:** Free
- Must be identified at time of reservation. Must board and exit with rider. Cannot be required but recommended for riders with cognitive conditions.
- **How to add:** Call 800-827-0829 to add PCA to your card
- **Finding an aide:** Personal Assistance Services Council (PASC) at 877-565-4477 can help locate aides

## Guests
- **Fare:** Same as rider fare
- One guest always allowed if space available. Additional guests only if room exists.

## Children
- **Under 6:** Free with eligible rider
- **Age 7+:** Pay fare as guest
- **Car seat:** Ages 5-8 or under 4'9" must use safety/booster seat (rider provides; driver may assist installation)

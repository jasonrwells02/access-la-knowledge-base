# Cancellation Policy

- **Advance required:** At least 2 hours before scheduled pickup time
- **How to cancel:** Call Reservations at 800-883-1295
- **Late cancellation:** Canceling less than 2 hours before pickup is recorded as a No Show
- **Drop-off/pickup:** Schedule as two separate trips. Driver cannot wait at a location.

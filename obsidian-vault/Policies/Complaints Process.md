# Complaints Process

## How to File
- **Phone:** 800-827-0829 (Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM)
- **TDD:** 800-827-1359
- **Email:** cserv@accessla.org
- **Mail:** Access Customer Service, PO Box 5728, El Monte, CA 91734
- **Web:** accessla.org/contactform

## Required Information
- Rider ID Number
- Full name, address, phone, email
- Filer name (if different from rider)
- Incident date, time, location
- Region or contractor involved
- Names of involved parties and witnesses
- Detailed summary (including pickup address, requested time, given time, actual arrival for pickup issues)

## Response Timeline
**14 calendar days (if response requested)**

## Rider360 Tracking
Registered web users can view complaint history and file new complaints at accessla.org/rider360

## Federal Escalation
- **Agency:** Federal Transit Administration, Office of Civil Rights
- **Phone:** 888-446-4511

> Riders may not experience retaliation for complaints. Access immediately investigates retaliation claims.

# Eligibility Process

## Basis
Based on the person with a disability's ability to use accessible buses and trains in LA County, not solely based on disability, age, or medical diagnosis.

## Categories
- **Unrestricted:** Eligible for all Access trips
- **Restricted:** Eligible for certain trip types based on functional limitations
- **Temporary:** Eligible with an expiration date

## 4-Step Process

### Step 1: Determine Eligibility
Download Evaluation Information and Application Packet from accessla.org (English/Spanish). Call 800-827-0829 for alternative formats.

### Step 2: Obtain Access ID Number
- Online at eligibility.accessla.org
- Phone: 800-827-0829 (option 1)
- TDD: 800-827-1359

### Step 3: Schedule Transit Evaluation
- Phone: 626-532-1616 (TDD: 626-532-1620)
- English and Spanish. Sign language interpretation available upon request.
- Duration: Process takes up to 4 hours total. Evaluation itself is approximately 45 minutes.
- **Bring:** Valid photo ID, Medical documents, reports, medication list explaining disability impact, Mobility device normally used in community

### Step 4: Approval Process
Written notification within 21 days

## Renewal
Renewal information mailed 60 days before expiration

## Appeal
- Deadline: Within 60 days of denial
- Request an appeal by calling Customer Service at 800-827-0829. Appeals form available in English/Spanish.

## ID Card
- Green Access Rider ID Card with ID number below name. Must be shown for every trip.
- **Accepted alternate IDs:** Driver's License, Passport, Consulate ID, Metro TAP Card, Military ID, School ID

> Cards cannot be shared. Sharing constitutes fraud punishable by criminal prosecution and eligibility loss.

# Holiday Schedule

## Observed Holidays
- New Year's Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (July 4th)
- Labor Day
- Veterans' Day
- Thanksgiving
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Day

> Service availability varies by region on holidays. Call Reservations at 800-883-1295 to check holiday service in your area.

## Santa Clarita
- **Sunday schedule:** New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day
- **No service:** Thanksgiving, Christmas

## Antelope Valley
- **No service:** Labor Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's Day

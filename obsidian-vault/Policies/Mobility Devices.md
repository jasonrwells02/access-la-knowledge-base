# Mobility Devices

| Specification | Limit |
|---|---|
| Max Width | 30 inches |
| Max Length | 48 inches |
| Max Weight | 600 lbs (rider + device) |

- **Standard:** WC19-compliant wheelchairs recommended
- **Securement:** Positioning belts offered for mobility device security. Seatbelts required (extensions available).
- **Device users:** Riders using wheelchairs/scooters automatically receive accessible vehicles
- **Non-device users:** Non-device-using riders may receive standard or accessible vehicles (cannot request specific types)

> Riders changing devices must notify Access Customer Service to ensure proper vehicle assignment

# Title VI

Access Services complies with Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, providing services without regard to race, color, or national origin.

## Filing a Complaint
- **Deadline:** Within 180 days of the alleged discriminatory act
- **Format:** Written and signed by complainant or representative, detailing facts and circumstances

## Processing Timeline
| Step | Timeframe |
|---|---|
| Acknowledgment | Within 10 days of receipt |
| Investigation | Within 60 days |
| Final Decision | Within 90 days (Executive Director provides written decision) |

## Appeal
Dissatisfied complainants may appeal to Federal Transit Administration Office of Civil Rights

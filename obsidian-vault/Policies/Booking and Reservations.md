# Booking and Reservations

## Advance Booking
- **Minimum:** 1 day in advance (next-day booking)
- **Maximum:** 3 days in advance
- **Same-day:** Not guaranteed, may be available based on capacity

## Reservation Hours
- **General:** 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, 7 days a week
- **Santa Clarita / Antelope Valley:** Monday through Saturday 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Sunday 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM

## Required Information
- Access Rider ID Number
- Exact pick-up and drop-off addresses
- Cross-streets or landmarks
- Mobility device specifications (if applicable)
- Service animal presence
- Number of traveling companions
- Desired pick-up time
- Phone number for call-outs
- Return trip time (must specifically request)

## One-Hour Window
Reservationists may offer pick-up times up to one hour before or after your requested time. If exact time unavailable, two alternatives are provided.

## Online Booking
- Portal: Rider360 at accessla.org or booking.accessla.org
- Regions: Eastern, Southern, West/Central, Antelope Valley
- Northern Region: Northern region uses WMR app only

## Return Trips
> Must be explicitly requested. Scheduling is NOT automatic.

## Standing Orders
Recurring trips for consistent schedules (work, school, church, dialysis)
- **Minimum commitment:** 6 weeks
- **Advance request:** 2+ weeks in advance
- **Schedule types:** Daily, Multiple-weekly, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly

# No-Show Policy

## Definition
A no-show is recorded when:
- Canceling less than 2 hours before scheduled pickup time
- Driver arrives within 20-minute window, waits 5 minutes, cannot find you, and obtains dispatcher approval to depart

## NOT a No-Show
- Driver arrives after the 20-minute window and rider cancels
- Driver arrives or departs before the scheduled pickup time
- Driver fails to wait the required 5 minutes

## Threshold
- **Count:** 5+ no-shows in a calendar month
- **Percentage:** Must exceed 10% of total trips

## Consequences
- **Warning:** Written notice after 2nd incident
- **First suspension:** 10 days
- **Subsequent:** 30 days
- **Clearance:** Suspension record clears after 6 months without additional violations

## Appeal
Contact Customer Service to explain circumstances or dispute decisions

## Prevention Tips
- Provide accurate pickup addresses
- Keep a reliable phone number on file
- Set reminders for your trips
- Be ready and visible at the curb before pickup time
- Use the Where's My Ride app to track your vehicle
- Ask family or caregivers for help with reminders

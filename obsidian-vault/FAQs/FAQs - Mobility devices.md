# FAQs: Mobility devices

## Can I bring my wheelchair or scooter?
Yes. Your mobility device must not exceed 30 inches wide by 48 inches long, and the combined weight of you and your device must not exceed 600 pounds. WC19-compliant wheelchairs are recommended. If you use a mobility device, you'll automatically receive an accessible vehicle. If you change devices, notify Customer Service to ensure the right vehicle is assigned.

## My wheelchair doesn't fit the size requirements. What can I do?
Contact the Reasonable Modification Coordinator, Torrance Johnson, at 213-270-6159 or email RMC@accessla.org to discuss options. You can request a reasonable modification to Access policies to accommodate your specific needs.

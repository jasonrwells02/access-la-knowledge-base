# FAQs: Booking

## How do I book a ride?
Call Reservations at 800-883-1295 and select your region. They're available from 6 AM to 10 PM, seven days a week. You can also book online through Rider360 at booking.accessla.org or through the Where's My Ride app. Trips should be booked 1 to 3 days in advance. Out-of-state callers dial 213-488-1748.

## Can I book a same-day trip?
Same-day trips are not guaranteed but may be available based on capacity. We recommend booking 1 to 3 days in advance to ensure availability.

## What information do I need to make a reservation?
You need your Access Rider ID Number, exact pickup and drop-off addresses, cross-streets or landmarks, mobility device details if applicable, whether you have a service animal, number of companions, desired pickup time, a phone number for call-outs, and your return trip time if you need one. Return trips must be specifically requested.

## How do I book online?
Set up a Rider360 account at accessla.org using your Rider ID number, eligibility expiration date, last name, and zip code. Then go to booking.accessla.org to book trips. Online booking is available for the Eastern, Southern, West/Central, and Antelope Valley regions. The Northern region can book via the Where's My Ride app only.

## What is a standing order?
A standing order is a recurring reservation for trips you take on a regular schedule, like going to work, school, church, or dialysis. Instead of calling each time, Access automatically schedules your trips. You need a minimum 6-week commitment and should request 2 or more weeks in advance. Contact Reservations at 800-883-1295, Option 3 to set one up.

## Why wasn't my return trip scheduled?
Return trips must be specifically requested when you make your reservation. They are not automatically scheduled. When booking, always tell the reservationist you need a return trip and provide the desired pickup time for your return.

## How do I set up rides for dialysis?
Dialysis is a great candidate for a standing order since it's a recurring trip. Contact Reservations at 800-883-1295 and select Option 3 after choosing your region. You'll need a minimum 6-week commitment. Your trips will be automatically scheduled for the same days and times each week. Different pickup times are allowed on different days.

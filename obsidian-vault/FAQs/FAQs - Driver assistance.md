# FAQs: Driver assistance

## What can the driver help me with?
Drivers assist with entering and exiting the vehicle, loading items, and limited help beyond the curb. They cannot enter buildings, provide medical care, or supervise riders. If you need help with memory, personal safety decisions, or personal/health needs, bring a Personal Care Attendant (rides free). If you need help beyond the curb, request Beyond the Curb service.

# FAQs: Regions

## What region am I in?
Access has six service regions: Antelope Valley (Lancaster, Palmdale area), Santa Clarita (Santa Clarita, Valencia, Newhall), Northern (San Fernando Valley, Burbank, Glendale, Pasadena), Eastern (El Monte, West Covina, Alhambra, Pomona), West/Central (Downtown LA, Hollywood, Santa Monica, Inglewood, South LA), and Southern (Gardena, Long Beach, Torrance, Compton, Downey, Whittier). When you call Reservations at 800-883-1295, the system will ask you to select your region.

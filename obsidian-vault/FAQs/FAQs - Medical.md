# FAQs: Medical

## Can I bring oxygen or medical equipment?
Yes, you can travel with oxygen and medical equipment. It must be secured and remain under your control during the trip. Plan ahead, as trip timing may not accommodate mid-trip medication needs. Travel time on Access is comparable to fixed-route bus service. If you need medical care during travel, bring a Personal Care Attendant.

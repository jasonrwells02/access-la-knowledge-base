# FAQs: Pickup

## When will my ride arrive?
Your vehicle may arrive within a 20-minute window around your scheduled pickup time. For example, if your pickup is scheduled for 10:00 AM, the vehicle may arrive between 9:40 AM and 10:20 AM. The driver will wait 5 minutes after arrival. Check your ride status by texting TRIP to 800-827-4588, using the Where's My Ride app, or calling the OMC at 800-827-0829 Option 2.

## My ride is late. What should I do?
Call the Operations Monitoring Center (OMC) at 800-827-0829 and press Option 2. They are available 24/7 and can check on your vehicle, provide an ETA, or secure an alternate ride. You can also text TRIP to 800-827-4588 for live agent assistance.

## How do I check on my ride status?
Three options: text the word TRIP to 800-827-4588 for live agent assistance, use the Where's My Ride app on your phone (available on iPhone and Android), or call Reservations at 800-883-1295, Option 4 (within 15 minutes of your scheduled pickup). The Where's My Ride app lets you track your vehicle in real time starting 15 minutes before your pickup.

## Why didn't I get a call-out notification?
Call-outs may not arrive if your phone number on file is incorrect, your cellular reception is poor, or your phone system requires pressing an additional number to accept the call. Contact Customer Service at 800-827-0829, Monday through Friday 8 AM to 5 PM, to verify your phone number. Cell phone numbers are recommended for the most reliable notifications.

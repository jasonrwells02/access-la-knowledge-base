# FAQs: Transit options

## What other transportation options are there besides Access?
LA County has many accessible transportation options. You can ride most fixed-route buses and trains for free with your Access card through the Free Fare Program. There are also local dial-a-ride services, Metro Micro on-demand rideshare, and over 200 public and private accessible providers. For help finding the right option, call our Mobility Management referral line at 800-431-7882, Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM.

## Does Access work with Uber or Lyft?
Access is an ADA paratransit service and does not partner with Uber or Lyft. However, if your scheduled Access vehicle is significantly delayed or misses your trip, the Operations Monitoring Center may arrange an alternate transportation provider, which could include a taxi or other backup service. For real-time help, call the OMC at 800-827-0829, Option 2.

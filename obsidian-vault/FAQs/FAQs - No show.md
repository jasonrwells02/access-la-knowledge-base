# FAQs: No show

## What is a no-show?
A no-show is recorded when you cancel less than 2 hours before your scheduled pickup time, or when the driver arrives within your 20-minute window, waits 5 minutes, and cannot find you. It is NOT a no-show if the driver arrives outside the 20-minute window, arrives or departs before your scheduled time, or doesn't wait the full 5 minutes.

## What happens if I get too many no-shows?
You'll receive a written notice after your 2nd no-show. If you have 5 or more no-shows in a calendar month AND they exceed 10% of your total trips that month, you may face suspension: 10 days for the first offense, 30 days for subsequent offenses. The suspension record clears after 6 months without violations. You can appeal by contacting Customer Service.

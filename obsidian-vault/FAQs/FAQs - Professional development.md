# FAQs: Professional development

## What professional development programs does Access offer?
Access Services' CTSA Extension provides subsidized technical training for LA County transit professionals through the Learning and Development Program. Over 500 professionals have participated. They also offer the Transit and Paratransit Management Certificate Program in partnership with University of the Pacific, requiring 7 of 8 courses. The Joe King Memorial Scholarship covers 100% of enrollment fees. Contact the University of the Pacific at 209-946-2956 or westgate@pacific.edu.

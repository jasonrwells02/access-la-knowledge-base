# FAQs: Technology

## Is there an app I can use?
Yes, the Where's My Ride app is available for iPhone (App Store) and Android (Google Play). It lets you track your vehicle in real time starting 15 minutes before your pickup. You can also book trips online through Rider360 at booking.accessla.org. For help, call the WMR hotline at 213-270-6099 or online reservations support at 213-270-6185.

## How do I use the Where's My Ride app?
Download the Access Services app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Log in with your Rider360 account credentials. You can check vehicle location 15 minutes before your scheduled pickup, manage your trip list, and communicate with your driver. You can also text TRIP to 800-827-4588 if you don't have the app.

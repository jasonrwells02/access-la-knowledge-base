# FAQs: Payment

## Can I pay with a credit card?
Yes, most regions accept VISA, MasterCard, and Discover cards. The one exception is the Santa Clarita region, which does not accept credit or debit cards. In Santa Clarita, you must pay with exact cash or coupon books. Drivers cannot make change anywhere, so always have exact fare ready.

# FAQs: Neighboring counties

## Can I travel to Orange County or other neighboring counties?
Yes. Call Access at 800-827-0829 at least two days in advance and request that we send your eligibility details to the neighboring county's paratransit service. You can use their service as a visitor for up to 21 days per calendar year. Neighboring services include OCTA Access (Orange County, 714-560-5956), Omnitrans Access (San Bernardino, 800-966-6428), Gold Coast Transit Access (Ventura, 888-667-7001), and RTA Dial-A-Ride (Riverside, 800-795-7887).

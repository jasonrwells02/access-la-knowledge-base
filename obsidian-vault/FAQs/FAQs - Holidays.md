# FAQs: Holidays

## Is Access available on holidays?
Service availability varies by region on holidays. Access observes New Year's Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Veterans' Day, Thanksgiving, the day after Thanksgiving, and Christmas. Standing orders are automatically cancelled on holidays. Regular service may still be available in most regions. Santa Clarita runs Sunday schedules on some holidays and has no service on Thanksgiving and Christmas. Call Reservations at 800-883-1295 to check holiday service in your area.

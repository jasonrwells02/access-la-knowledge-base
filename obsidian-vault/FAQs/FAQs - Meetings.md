# FAQs: Meetings

## When are the public meetings?
Board of Directors meets the 4th Monday of each month from 12 PM to 3 PM. Community Advisory Committee meets the 2nd Tuesday of each month from 1 PM to 3:15 PM. Both are hybrid (in-person and virtual via Zoom). TPAC meets the 2nd Thursday every other month from 9:30 to 10:30 AM. All meetings are at 3449 Santa Anita Ave, El Monte. Call 213-270-6110 for updates.

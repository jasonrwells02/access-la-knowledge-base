# FAQs: Emergency

## What should I do in a medical emergency?
Call 911 immediately. The Operations Monitoring Center is not equipped to handle medical emergencies. For mandatory evacuations, also call 911. The OMC at 800-827-0829 Option 2 can help with non-emergency trip issues 24/7.

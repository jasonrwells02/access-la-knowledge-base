# FAQs: Companions

## Can someone ride with me?
Yes. A Personal Care Attendant (PCA) rides free but must be identified when making your reservation. You may bring one guest who pays the same fare as you. Additional guests are space-available only. Children under 6 ride free on a parent's lap. Children 6 and older need their own seat and pay the fare. To add a PCA to your card, call 800-827-0829.

## How do I find a personal care attendant?
The Personal Assistance Services Council (PASC) at 877-565-4477 can help you locate personal care aides. They provide referrals for qualified attendants who can accompany you on Access trips.

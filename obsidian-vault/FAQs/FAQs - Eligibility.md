# FAQs: Eligibility

## Who is eligible for Access?
Eligibility depends on your functional ability to independently use fixed-route bus and train service for all or part of your trips. You may be eligible if you cannot independently get to or from a bus stop, board or exit a bus, understand which bus to take or when to exit, or navigate schedules due to a disability. Functional disabilities include cognitive, mental, psychiatric, visual, auditory, physical, or medical conditions. Being able to drive does not disqualify you.

## How do I apply for Access?
Call Customer Service at 800-827-0829, Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 5 PM, or visit the website application at eligibility.accessla.org. The process has four steps: download the application packet, get an Access ID number (online or by phone), schedule an in-person transit evaluation at 626-532-1616, and receive a written decision within 21 days.

## How do I prepare for my eligibility evaluation?
Identify and note any barriers you experience when getting to bus stops, boarding, or interacting with schedules. Describe how your disability affects these activities. Bring a valid photo ID, medical documents or reports explaining your disability impact, and any mobility device you normally use. The evaluation takes about 45 minutes, but plan for up to 4 hours total. Free transportation to and from the evaluation center is available.

## What if I'm found not eligible?
You can request an appeal within 60 days by calling Customer Service at 800-827-0829. An appeals form is available in English and Spanish on the website. Alternative transportation options also exist, and Mobility Management staff can help you explore them.

## How do I renew my eligibility?
Renewal information is mailed to you 60 days before your eligibility expires. Make sure your address and phone number are current. If you haven't received your renewal notice, call Customer Service at 800-827-0829, Option 3 for Eligibility.

## Can I use the service while waiting for my card or renewal?
Yes. Contact Customer Service at 800-827-0829, Option 4, to request authorization to ride using a valid government-issued ID while waiting for your card. Accepted IDs include a Driver's License, Passport, Consulate ID, Metro TAP Card, Military ID, or School ID.

## Can I apply with a temporary disability?
Yes. Call Customer Service at 800-827-0829, Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 5 PM, for information about applying with a temporary disability.

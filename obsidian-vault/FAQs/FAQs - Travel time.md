# FAQs: Travel time

## How long will my trip take?
Because Access is a shared-ride service, routes may include picking up and dropping off other passengers. Estimated travel times: 1-10 miles takes 30 minutes to 1.5 hours, 11-20 miles takes 1-2.5 hours, 21-30 miles takes 1.5-3 hours, and over 30 miles takes 2-3.5+ hours. Per ADA law, trips may take as long as a comparable bus/train trip.

# FAQs: Stands

## What are Access stands?
Access has 47 designated pick-up and drop-off stands at popular locations like hospitals, colleges, airports, and entertainment venues. They work like bus stops. Some key locations include LAX, Union Station, Cedars-Sinai, VA Hospitals, Cal State universities, and venues like SoFi Stadium and Universal Studios. You can specify a stand as your pick-up or drop-off point when booking. Visit accessla.org for the full searchable directory.

# FAQs: Visitor

## I'm visiting LA. Can I use Access?
Yes. If you're ADA paratransit eligible in your home area, you can use Access for up to 21 days per calendar year. Provide proof of eligibility from your local paratransit provider, or proof of address outside LA County with disability documentation. Submit by email to cserv@accessla.org, fax to 213-270-6057, or mail. Processing is within one business day. Call 800-827-0829, Option 6 to book.

## How do I travel to a neighboring county?
Call 800-827-0829 at least two days in advance and request that Access send your eligibility details to the other county's paratransit provider. Most paratransit providers allow up to 21 days per calendar year as a visitor.

# FAQs: Business

## How do I become a vendor or contractor for Access?
Register as a vendor through the Doing Business With Us section at accessla.org. For procurement inquiries, contact purchasing@accessla.org or call 213-270-6000. Access uses IFBs, RFPs, RFIQs, and RFQs for solicitations. For the Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) program, apply through the California Unified Certification Program at californiaucp.dbesystem.com.

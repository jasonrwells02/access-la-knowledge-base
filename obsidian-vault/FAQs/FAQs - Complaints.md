# FAQs: Complaints

## How do I file a complaint?
Call Customer Service at 800-827-0829 (Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM), email cserv@accessla.org, send a letter to PO Box 5728, El Monte, CA 91734, or use the online contact form at accessla.org. You'll need your Rider ID, details of the incident including date, time, and location, and any names of people involved. Access responds within 14 calendar days.

## Can I file a discrimination complaint?
Yes. Contact the ADA Coordinator at 213-270-6000 or email adacoordinator@accessla.org. Complaints must be filed within 180 days in writing. Access acknowledges receipt within 10 days, investigates within 60 days, and provides a final decision within 90 days. You may also file with the Federal Transit Administration at 888-446-4511.

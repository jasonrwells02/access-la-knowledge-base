# FAQs: Fares

## How much does a ride cost?
One-way fares are $2.75 for trips under 20 miles and $3.50 for trips 20 miles or more. Trips within Santa Clarita or the Antelope Valley are $2.00. Personal Care Attendants ride free. Guests pay the same fare as the rider. You can pay with exact cash, credit/debit card (VISA, MasterCard, Discover), or coupon books. Drivers cannot make change.

## How do I buy coupon books?
Coupon books come in three types: Base Fare ($27.50 for 10 coupons at $2.75 each), Plus Zone ($7.50 for 10 coupons at $0.75 each for trips over 20 miles), and Flex ($20.00 for 10 coupons at $2.00 each for Santa Clarita/Antelope Valley). Order by mail with a check/money order (allow 10 days), online at accessla.org, or at select locations in person.

## What payment methods are accepted?
Exact cash (drivers cannot make change), credit/debit cards (VISA, MasterCard, Discover) except in Santa Clarita, and coupon books. Coupons are non-refundable and Access is not responsible for lost or stolen coupons.

# FAQs: Rules

## Can I eat or drink on the vehicle?
Eating and drinking are not allowed on Access vehicles unless it is medically necessary. If you have a medical condition that requires eating or drinking during your trip, that is permitted.

## Is smoking or vaping allowed?
No. Smoking and vaping are prohibited on all Access vehicles.

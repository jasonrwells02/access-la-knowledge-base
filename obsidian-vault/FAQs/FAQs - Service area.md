# FAQs: Service area

## What areas does Access serve?
Access serves within 3/4 mile of fixed-route bus lines and Metro Rail stations throughout Los Angeles County, extending into portions of San Bernardino, Orange, and Ventura counties. The service area spans over 1,950 square miles from Lancaster to Long Beach and Malibu to Montclair. It does not cover commuter rail or commuter bus routes.

## What are the service regions?
Access has 6 service regions: Antelope Valley (served by First Transit), Northern (MV Transportation), Eastern (San Gabriel Transit), Southern (Global Paratransit), West/Central (California Transit), and Santa Clarita (City of Santa Clarita). When you call Reservations, you select your region.

# Visitor Program

ADA paratransit service for visitors from outside Los Angeles County who cannot use accessible fixed-route transit due to disability-related functional limitations.

## Eligibility
- Unable to use accessible fixed-route due to disability-related functional limitations
- OR present ADA paratransit eligibility documentation from outside LA County
- OR provide out-of-county residence proof and disability documentation









## Duration
Up to 21 days within one calendar year (365 days from first trip). Travel days do not have to be consecutive.

**Fare:** Standard Access fare



## Registration
- Processing: Within one business day from receipt

# Travel Training

Free one-on-one individualized training that gives people with disabilities the skills required to travel safely on fixed-route public transportation.

## Eligibility
All individuals who have completed the Access Services eligibility process. Participation does not affect your eligibility status.





## Skills Taught
- Schedule interpretation for buses and trains
- Fare payment methods
- Boarding and exiting procedures
- Transfer techniques
- Signaling drivers for stops or assistance
- Wheelchair and mobility device securing
- Safety awareness and decision-making
- Life skills management
- Handling service disruptions
- Social and communication skills





**Fare:** Standard Access fare

# Beyond the Curb (BTC)

Origin-to-destination assistance where the driver accompanies the rider up to 60 feet from the vehicle. Available as a reasonable modification for riders who cannot navigate from curb to their destination independently.

## Eligibility
Riders whose disability prevents them from using curb-to-curb paratransit for a specific trip. New applicants evaluated during initial eligibility screening. Existing customers apply via Reasonable Modification Request Form.


## How to Request
- Request during trip reservation (for approved riders)
- Call 800-827-0829, press #6 to request a Reasonable Modification Form
- Email RMC@accessla.org
- Grace period: current customers can request during reservations while applications are under review
## Scope
- Distance: Up to 60 feet from vehicle
- Driver must maintain visual contact with vehicle
- Max dwell time: 5 minutes
## Limitations
- Driver cannot enter buildings, residences, or private doors
- Cannot assist with oversized packages
- Cannot help beyond ground-floor levels
- Not available in alleys, driveways, or gated communities
- Driver may refuse if deemed unsafe






**Fare:** Standard Access fare (no additional charge)

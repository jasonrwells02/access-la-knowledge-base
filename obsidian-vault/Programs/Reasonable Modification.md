# Reasonable Modification

A modification to Access policies, practices, or procedures requested by an eligible customer to ensure they can use the paratransit system.

## Eligibility
undefined


## How to Request
- Call Customer Service at 800-827-0829, press #6
- Download Reasonable Modification Request Form from accessla.org
- Contact Reasonable Modification Coordinator at 213-270-6159
- Email RMC@accessla.org



## Denial Criteria
- Would fundamentally alter the nature of Access services, programs, or activities
- Would create a direct threat to health or safety of others
- Customer can fully use services without the modification
- Would cause unreasonable financial or administrative burden




**Fare:** Standard Access fare

# Parents with Disabilities Program (PWD)

Assists Access-certified parents/guardians of children age 16 and younger with reliable transportation to and from school and school-related activities.

## Eligibility
Access-certified riders who are parents or guardians of children 16 and younger

## Benefits
- Trip pickup times based on school schedule to prevent tardiness
- Linked trips: parent stays aboard after drop-off/pick-up to reach next destination
- Minimum 30-minute intervals between same-day trips for school escort time
- Up to 4 one-way same-day emergency trips monthly (9 AM to 7 PM)
- Service hours: 6 AM to 9 PM, 7 days a week









**Fare:** $2.00
